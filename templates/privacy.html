Privacy Policy for Internet Speed Tester

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Technical Data: We automatically gather information related to your internet connection and device, which may include:
    • IP Address: Used to determine your approximate location.
    • Download Speed: Measured during the speed test.
    • Upload Speed: Also measured during the speed test.
    • Latency (Ping): Measured to assess the response time of your internet connection.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our Service, including:
    • The duration of your speed tests
    • Interaction patterns with the application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Service
  • To monitor the performance of our Service and analyze usage trends
  • To display your internet speed results and latency information
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activities

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Service may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. If you choose to decline cookies, some parts of the Service may not function properly.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to legal requests or prevent harm
  • To protect our rights and property, and the safety of our users

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to rectify any inaccuracies
  • The right to request the deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page, with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
